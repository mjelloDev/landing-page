Capybara facts

Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world, weighing up to 150 pounds (68 kilograms).

They live in large groups called herds and are very social animals.

Capybaras are excellent swimmers and have webbed feet.

They have a symbiotic relationshipwith birds that clean parasites off their fur.
